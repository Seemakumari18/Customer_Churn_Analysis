## Customer_Churn_Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn data using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to identify the factors responsible for customer churn and provide business recommendations to improve customer retention.

## 💡 Key Business Insights
- Customers with Month-to-Month contracts have the highest churn.
- Customers with low tenure are more likely to leave.
- Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn.
- Certain cities have significantly higher churn rates.
- Customer support interactions can indicate churn risk.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendations
- Offer long-term contract discounts.
- Improve onboarding for new customers.
- Provide retention offers for high-risk customers.
- Monitor customers with frequent support calls.
- Launch loyalty and engagement programs.
